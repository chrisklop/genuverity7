# Deep Research Prompts: Disinformation Pipeline

**Purpose**: Upload this file to an LLM Project for systematic research on disinformation topics. Each prompt is designed to produce unbiased, comprehensive analysis suitable for fact-check reports and timeline articles.

**Usage**: Copy individual prompts or sections into your research workflow. Each produces structured output ready for GenuVerity report generation.

## Historical Disinformation Campaigns (1890s-1950s)

### 1. Yellow Journalism and the Spanish-American War
```
Research the role of yellow journalism in precipitating the Spanish-American War (1898). Include:
- Key figures: William Randolph Hearst, Joseph Pulitzer
- Specific fabricated or exaggerated stories (USS Maine coverage)
- Circulation war tactics and their influence on public opinion
- Congressional and presidential responses
- Long-term impact on American journalism ethics
- Primary sources: newspaper archives, congressional records
Maintain neutral analysis of competing historical interpretations.
```

### 2. Committee on Public Information (CPI) - WWI Propaganda
```
Analyze the Committee on Public Information (1917-1919) as America's first large-scale government propaganda operation. Cover:
- George Creel's leadership and organizational structure
- Four-Minute Men program and its reach
- Visual propaganda: James Montgomery Flagg, Howard Chandler Christy posters
- Film division and "The Kaiser, the Beast of Berlin"
- Suppression tactics and the Espionage/Sedition Acts
- Post-war dissolution and lessons learned
- Scholarly debate on its effectiveness and ethics
```

### 3. The Protocols of the Elders of Zion
```
Document the creation, spread, and debunking of "The Protocols of the Elders of Zion." Include:
- Origins in Russian secret police (Okhrana) fabrication

- Nazi adoption and amplification
- Times of London exposé (1921)
- Modern persistence on social media
- Academic consensus on its fraudulent nature
Present factually without amplifying antisemitic content.
```

### 4. Nazi Propaganda Apparatus

- Radio as mass medium: Volksempfänger distribution

### 5. Soviet Disinformation Operations (Active Measures)
