## 2. The Minab School Attack: Verified Facts

### What Happened
- **Date:** February 28, 2026 (first day of strikes)
- **Location:** Shajareh Tayyebeh girls' elementary school, Minab, Hormozgan province, southern Iran
- **Victims:** 165-170+ killed, ~95 injured — mostly girls aged 7-12
- **Method:** Triple-tapped by three missiles during school day
- **Sequence:** After first strike, principal moved students to prayer room; second strike hit that shelter; parent received call about first strike but daughter killed in second before he could arrive

### Responsibility Investigation
- **CNN analysis:** Evidence points to US Tomahawk Land Attack Missile (TLAM) striking adjacent IRGC naval base
- **Snopes:** "There's no proof Iran bombed Minab girls' school. Evidence points to likely US role"
- **Al Jazeera investigation:** Targeting "likely deliberate"
- **Human Rights Watch:** Called for investigation as war crime
- **UN/OHCHR:** Condemned the strike, called for independent investigation
- **UNESCO:** "Deadly bombing of Iran primary school 'a grave violation of humanitarian law'"
- **US/Israel position:** Denied involvement; Trump claimed "the destruction was caused by Iran itself"

### Relevance to Propaganda Video
The "Narrative of Victory" uses the Minab school attack as emotional fuel — showing dead children and a vengeful soldier. This is **based on a real event with verified casualties**, making it more powerful than pure fabrication. The propaganda element is the narrative framing (100% US blame, no IRGC base context) rather than the underlying facts.

## 3. Military Claims Verification: Iran's "Narrative of Victory"

### Claim: Iran Can Destroy US Aircraft Carriers

**Assessment: ASPIRATIONAL but not entirely fabricated**

**Iran's Anti-Ship Arsenal:**
- **Khalij Fars:** Anti-ship ballistic missile, ~300km range, derived from Fateh-110 family, electro-optical seeker for terminal guidance
- **Coastal defense batteries:** C-701 Kosar, C-704 Nasr, C-802 Noor, C-802A Ghader, HY-2 Seersucker
- **Pending:** Near deal with China for CM-302 supersonic anti-ship cruise missiles (Fox News)
- **IRGC Navy:** 20,000+ naval personnel, ~5,000 marines, swarm boats under 10 tonnes

**Iran's Strategy:**
- Layered anti-access/area denial (A2/AD) centered on mines, submarines, anti-ship missiles, swarm craft
- **5,000-6,000 naval mines** in stockpile — disproportionately disruptive to clear
- Strategy is **attrition, not decisive victory** — stretching US defenses and political tolerance

**Pentagon Assessment:**
- A successful attack on a US Carrier Strike Group is "exceedingly difficult" due to layered CSG defenses
- However, the threat is not zero — asymmetric swarm tactics + anti-ship ballistic missiles represent a genuine challenge
- **Army Recognition:** "Iran builds layered missile and mine shield against U.S. carriers in Strait of Hormuz"

**Verdict:** The video showing a US carrier being destroyed is **propaganda fantasy** as a definitive outcome, but Iran's capability to **damage** carrier operations in the confined Strait is taken seriously by Pentagon planners.

### Claim: Iran Controls the Strait of Hormuz

**Assessment: PARTIALLY TRUE — de facto interdiction capability, not sovereignty**

- Strait narrows to ~21 nautical miles at tightest point
- Iran's coastline dominates the northern shore
- **2026 Strait of Hormuz crisis:** IRGC issued warnings prohibiting vessel passage during the conflict
- International shipping disrupted — "No ships in the Strait of Hormuz" scenario analyzed by Navy Lookout
- Iran demonstrated ability to **disrupt** but not permanently **control** the strait

### Claim: Iran Successfully Struck US/Allied Targets

**Assessment: TRUE — verified strikes occurred**

- **Shuaiba port, Kuwait:** 6 US soldiers killed, 18 wounded (verified)
- **US Embassy Riyadh:** Hit by 2 drones, "minor material damage" (verified)
- **Multiple Gulf states:** Iran struck targets in Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, UAE (verified)
- **CIA station hit:** Washington Post reported CIA station in Saudi capital hit in drone attack

### Claim: Epstein Connection to Iran Strikes

**Assessment: FABRICATED NARRATIVE**

The video opens with Trump reading Epstein files and implies he ordered Iran strikes as a distraction. There is **no evidence** linking the timing of Operation Epic Fury to Epstein disclosures. The operation was a coordinated US-Israel military campaign with strategic objectives (nuclear program, regime change), not a domestic scandal distraction.
