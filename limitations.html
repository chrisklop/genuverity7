Known Limitations

Where our system stops, and where human judgment begins.

Data Horizons

We cannot verify what we cannot see. While we scan 50,000+ sources, significant portions of the world's knowledge remain offline or gated.

  • Hard Paywalls: Premium financial data is often inaccessible.
  • Offline Records: Non-digitized county archives are invisible.
  • Private Channels: Signal/Telegram chats are out of scope.

The Nuance Gap

Large Language Models are literalists. They struggle to detect satire, deep sarcasm, and cultural nuance. A statement like "Yeah, right" may be interpreted as confirmation rather than skepticism.

Visual Blindspots

We currently verify text only. In an era of Generative AI, this is a significant limitation.

  • Deepfake Videos
