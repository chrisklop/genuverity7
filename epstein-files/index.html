What We're Building

Credibility Badges

Every document rated A through F based on source type. Court records treated differently than unverified tips.

Semantic Document Search

Vector-powered search across the DOJ document corpus. Find relevant documents by meaning, not just keywords.

Misinformation Alerts

Automatic warnings when a search topic has known false claims circulating, linked to our published fact-checks.

Local backup of all released documents, including files removed from the DOJ portal after initial release.
