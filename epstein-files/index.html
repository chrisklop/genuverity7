This section is under active development. The features below are what we're working toward.

What We're Building

Credibility Badges

Every document rated A through F based on source type. Court records (A) treated differently than unverified tips (D).

Document Search

Semantic search across the DOJ document corpus, powered by vector embeddings and our existing analysis tools.

Misinformation Alerts

Automatic warnings when a search topic has known false claims circulating. Linked to our published fact-checks.

Preserved Archive

Local backup of all released documents, including files that were removed from the DOJ portal after initial release.

Credibility Badge System

Every document in the hub will carry one of these badges so you can immediately assess source reliability.

Court Record Official court filings, depositions, judicial orders
Government DOJ, FBI, or other official government documents
Verified Media Confirmed by multiple credible news sources
Unverified Tips or claims awaiting independent verification
News Report Single source, not independently verified
Flagged Known or suspected misinformation/fabrication
