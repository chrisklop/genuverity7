What is GenuVerity?

GenuVerity is an AI-powered fact-checking platform that will automatically track viral stories as they spread across social media, then generate comprehensive, source-heavy investigative reports in real-time.

When a claim goes viral, we dig into it immediately, pulling from primary sources, official documents, and verified data, then publish our findings back to social platforms where the conversation is happening.
