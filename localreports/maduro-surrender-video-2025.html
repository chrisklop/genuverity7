FALSE

A video purporting to show Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro announcing his surrender was completely fake. Forensic analysis confirmed it was an AI-generated deepfake. Maduro remained in power and never made any such announcement. The video spread false hope among Venezuelan opposition supporters.

Executive Summary

In 2025, a video circulated on social media showing what appeared to be Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro announcing he was stepping down and surrendering power. The video was entirely fabricated using AI deepfake technology. Forensic analysis revealed telltale signs of synthesis, and Maduro continued to govern Venezuela. The fake spread primarily among diaspora communities and opposition supporters, causing confusion and false hope.

The Fake Video

A video showed what appeared to be Maduro announcing surrender in a televised address [1].

Forensic Analysis

Reality Check

Conclusion
