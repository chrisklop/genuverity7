FALSE

Images showing convicted Unnao rape case perpetrator Kuldeep Singh Sengar being garlanded outside Tihar Jail were AI-generated fakes. BOOM Live and Alt News identified the images using multiple AI detection tools including Hive Moderation and Sightengine. Sengar remains imprisoned - he was not released. Executive Summary

In late 2025, images spread on social media purporting to show former BJP MLA Kuldeep Singh Sengar - convicted in the notorious Unnao rape case - being garlanded by supporters outside prison. The images sparked outrage and were shared by some politicians. BOOM Live and Alt News quickly identified the images as AI-generated using multiple detection tools. Prison authorities confirmed Sengar remained incarcerated. This case shows how AI can weaponize real crimes to create false outrage-generating content.

The Viral Images

The images showed a man resembling Sengar wearing garlands, surrounded by supporters, with jail buildings visible in the background [1].

Captions claimed he had been released and was being "welcomed like a hero" - designed to provoke outrage [4].

AI Detection Analysis

BOOM Live ran the images through multiple AI detection tools. Hive Moderation returned a 97.8% probability of AI generation [1].

Alt News identified telltale AI artifacts including distorted hands, inconsistent shadows, and unnatural background blending [2].

Sengar's Actual Status

Kuldeep Singh Sengar was convicted in 2019 and sentenced to life imprisonment for the rape of a minor. He remains incarcerated [3].

Prison authorities confirmed no release had occurred. Court records show his appeals have been rejected [9].

Conclusion

The images of Kuldeep Singh Sengar being garlanded upon release are AI-generated fabrications. He remains in prison serving a life sentence. This case demonstrates how AI image generation can weaponize real crimes and real victims' trauma to create politically inflammatory false content.
